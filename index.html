About

Kausar Ali Chaudhary is a Ph.D. researcher and Visvesvaraya Fellow in the Department of Computer Science at Aligarh Muslim University. His research interests include Artificial Intelligence, Randomized Neural Networks (RdNNs), Artificial Neural Networks (ANNs), Fuzzy Systems, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Bioinformatics. His work focuses on developing novel neural architectures, loss functions, activation functions, and optimization strategies to enhance learning efficiency, scalability, robustness, and interpretability. He is particularly interested in integrating fuzzy intelligence with neural computing to address uncertainty in complex real-world problems.

Kausar has published research in IEEE conferences and is actively pursuing high-impact contributions in leading journals, particularly IEEE Transactions on Fuzzy Systems. His long-term goal is to advance the theoretical foundations and practical applications of intelligent systems for healthcare, bioinformatics, and data-driven scientific discovery.
